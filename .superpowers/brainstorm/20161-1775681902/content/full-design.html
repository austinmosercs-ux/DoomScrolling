Escalating Commentary System

// Phase 1: Subtle (posts 1-15)
━━━ POST 5 OF ∞ ━━━
━━━ POST 12 OF ∞ ━━━
// Phase 2: Aware (posts 16-35)
━━━ YOU'VE SEEN 20 POSTS. NONE OF THEM MATTERED. ━━━
━━━ POST 28. STILL HERE? ━━━
// Phase 3: Aggressive (posts 36+)
⚠ YOU HAVE BEEN HERE FOR 5 MINUTES. THIS IS THE PROBLEM. ⚠
⚠ CLOSE THE TAB. GO OUTSIDE. DO LITERALLY ANYTHING ELSE. ⚠
⚠ THIS IS POST 50. YOU WILL NOT REMEMBER A SINGLE ONE. ⚠

Infinite Scroll Behavior

• IntersectionObserver watches a sentinel element at the bottom
• When visible → generate 5-8 new posts, append to feed
• Posts are mix of image + text (using existing 10 images, cycling randomly)
• Commentary dividers injected every ~5 posts
• Phase escalates based on total post count + time on page
• No button, no page reload — just endless content
• Bottom nav simplified: just "← back" link to index.html
