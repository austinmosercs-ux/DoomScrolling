How Ads Differ From Regular Posts

• Red border instead of white (marks it as an ad)
• SPONSORED badge in top-right corner
• White square avatar with brand initials instead of red
• "PROMOTED" label under brand name instead of timestamp
• Ad copy in ALL CAPS (aggressive, on-brand)
• SHOP NOW ► button at the bottom
• Brand URL shown (fake, non-clickable)
• 10% chance any post is an ad
